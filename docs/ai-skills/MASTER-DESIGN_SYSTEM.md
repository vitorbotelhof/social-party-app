# ENTRE NÓS — MASTER DESIGN SYSTEM

## PRODUCT ESSENCE

Entre Nós is an intimate social game platform for couples and close friends.

The app should feel:
- emotionally warm
- playful
- seductive
- modern
- premium
- safe
- elegant
- not childish
- not overly sexualized

The emotional tone should feel like:
- late night conversations
- intimacy
- emotional tension
- discovery
- flirtation
- curiosity

Avoid:
- gamer aesthetics
- neon overload
- generic party game visuals
- loud interfaces
- excessive gradients
- cheap dating app feeling

---

## UX PRINCIPLES

### 1. One action per screen
The experience must feel effortless.

### 2. Large tap targets
Mobile-first always.

### 3. Emotional pacing
The app should create anticipation.

### 4. Reduce cognitive load
No clutter.

### 5. Focus on immersion
Transitions and spacing matter more than decorations.

---

## VISUAL STYLE

Inspired by:
- Apple Human Interface
- Airbnb
- Calm
- modern relationship apps
- luxury editorial design

Typography:
- clean
- elegant
- high readability
- generous spacing

Use:
- large typography
- breathing room
- soft contrast

Avoid:
- dense UI
- hard borders
- sharp shadows
- excessive cards

---

## COLOR SYSTEM

Primary:
Warm dark tones.

Accent:
Soft romantic highlight color.

Avoid:
Pure black.
Harsh red.
Over-saturated pink.

---

## COMPONENT BEHAVIOR

Buttons:
- large
- minimal
- smooth radius
- premium motion

Cards:
- immersive
- edge-to-edge feeling

Animations:
- subtle
- cinematic
- fluid
- 200–300ms

---

## PRODUCT GOAL

Users should feel:
"I want to keep playing and connecting."
