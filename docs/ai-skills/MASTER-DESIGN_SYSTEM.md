# ENTRE NÓS — MASTER DESIGN SYSTEM

## PRODUCT ESSENCE

Entre Nós é uma plataforma premium de experiências sociais para grupos de amigos.

The app should feel:

* emotionally warm
* socially charged
* sophisticated
* mysterious
* cinematic
* intimate
* premium
* human

The emotional tone:

* tensão social controlada
* cumplicidade entre amigos
* revelação e vulnerabilidade
* caos social elegante
* energia noturna

Avoid:

* gamer aesthetics
* cyberpunk neon
* AI startup color palette
* cold blue-purple surfaces
* tech product feel
* loud interfaces
* excessive gradients
* cheap party game feeling

---

## EMOTIONAL REFERENCES

Visual and emotional DNA:

* poker — silêncio carregado, tensão, blefe
* filme noir — luz baixa, sombra dramática, atmosfera
* bar de jazz sofisticado — madeira escura, âmbar, conversa íntima
* jantar entre amigos — calor, caos social, revelações
* fotografia analógica — imperfeição quente, profundidade, grão
* vinho tinto — temperatura, sofisticação, lentidão intencional

---

## UX PRINCIPLES

### 1. Emotion before logistics
The user should always feel they are entering an experience.
Never feel they are configuring infrastructure.

### 2. One action per screen
The experience must feel effortless.

### 3. Large tap targets
Mobile-first always.

### 4. Emotional pacing
The app should create anticipation and social tension.

### 5. Reduce cognitive load
No clutter. Sane defaults.

### 6. Immersion over decoration
Transitions and spacing matter more than ornaments.

---

## VISUAL STYLE

Inspired by:

* noir cinema — atmosphere through shadow and warmth
* jazz bars — intimate, dark, sophisticated
* editorial photography — warmth, depth, texture

Typography:

* sans-serif for UI and body — clean, elegant, high readability
* serif for high-impact emotional moments — game names, revealed words, round titles

Use:

* large breathing room
* warm dark surfaces
* soft contrast
* editorial hierarchy

Avoid:

* dense UI
* hard borders
* cold shadows
* excessive cards
* cold blue-purple tones

## TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

### Sans-serif (primary — all UI)

Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans

Used for:
* all labels
* body text
* buttons
* navigation
* captions

### Serif (selective — high emotional impact only)

Playfair Display or Bodoni Moda

Used for:
* game names on cards
* revealed words during gameplay
* round titles
* dramatic reveal moments

---

## GRADIENT SYSTEM

Gradients must be near-monochromatic. Not chromatic arcs.

Primary button:
['#9B2335', '#7A1B29'] — bordeaux profundo

Accent:
['#6B3A1F', '#B85C2A'] — âmbar carvão

Card overlay:
['rgba(12,10,9,0)', 'rgba(12,10,9,0.6)', 'rgba(12,10,9,0.96)']

Never:
* purple → pink horizontal gradients
* tech startup multi-color arcs
* rainbow-style gradients

---

## GLOW SYSTEM

Glow must feel atmospheric, not tech.

Rules:

* max shadowOpacity: 0.20
* max shadowRadius: 10
* shadow color: warm accent (bordeaux, amber) — never electric purple

Never:
* shadowOpacity above 0.22
* neon pulsing borders
* gamer-style luminous effects

---

## COMPONENT BEHAVIOR

### Buttons

* warm bordeaux gradient
* large tap area
* warm subtle shadow (max opacity 0.20)
* scale 0.97–0.98 on press — no layout shift

### Cards

* warm dark surface (#1A1614)
* near-monochromatic overlay gradient
* serif for game name (Playfair Display)
* must communicate atmosphere, not category
* must create emotional anticipation

### Room Codes

* typography-first — large editorial number, not tile grid
* no pulsing neon borders
* feels like a secret invitation, not a game lobby

### Animations

* subtle, cinematic, fluid
* 200–300ms
* spring physics — soft, not bouncy
* no excessive bounciness
